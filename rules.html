Rules:

  1. The first one with no cards left wins.
  2. If you can not make a correct move, draw a card.
  3. If your oppenet puts a card with a number "2" on the table you take 2 cards and miss a turn.
  4. If your oppenet puts a card with a number "3" on the table you take 3 cards and miss a turn.
  5. If your oppenet puts a card with a number "4" on the table you miss a turn.
  6. You can put any "Q" card on any card on the table.
  7. If your oppenet puts a "K♠" or "K♥" on the table you take 5 cards and miss a turn.
